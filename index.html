Tom Robinson case overview
  • Defendant: Tom Robinson — A hardworking and kindhearted Father

Why YOU should support Tom

As sensible citizens of Maycomb, we have to recognize that Tom Robinson was falsely prosecuted solely because of his skin color and the racial bias that poisons our town. As our founding fathers declared, "all men are created equal." It is our moral and civic duty to stand up for that truth and to demand the freedom of Tom Robinson.

Trust & Credibility

Atticus Finch, the elected representative for Maycomb County in the Alabama State Legislature and a man known for his unwavering integrity and honesty risks everything to defend Tom Robinson. Atticus would never violate his moral integrity to defend a guilty man. So if you don't believe in Tom at least believe in Atticus

Tom's humanity

Tom Robinson has shown kindness consistently through his actions such as helping Mayella Ewell with chores out of compassion and pity. Beyond being a kind soul he is a loving husband and father. Imagine Helen and their children struggling to survive day to day without the care and presence of their father and husband.

Solid facts

Evidence from the trial raised questions about the legitimacy of th Ewell's testimony. Mayella Ewell was beaten on the right side but Toms left arm was crippled due to an accident but suspiciously Bob Ewell writes his name in court with his left hand. Along with physical evidence there were several contradictions in the testimonies of Mayella and Bob Ewell
